How to draw animals from the wild





The wildlife! Find out how to draw animals with big teeth, long necks and very bad attitude. Your not to the farm anymore... you're now struggling for your life!

Wild animals bring a new dimension to explore for an artist. Yes, like you did with the farm animals, you can also draw a cute snake or a funny elephant.



But it's also more challenging and rewarding to try to depict the aggressive nature of a snake, a lion or any other wild animal. Cartoons can look funny or dramatic. You call the shot!

This 4 step method is really simple to understand. First, you will observe a real image of the chosen animal. Then, I will show you the basic shapes that compose the drawing structure of this animal. The third step consist of learning how to draw a simple character. Finally, you will have the chance to test your skill by drawing several types of characters until you find your own style!

You can always use a simple drawing software to make some nice vector drawings with your illustrations. This kind of graphic art is easy to do and will bring a professional look to your artwork.

So don't worry, just follow these easy examples, start sketching and you will have all the tools required to learn how to draw strong animals from the wild!

Start practicing now by choosing your favorite subject on your right!
