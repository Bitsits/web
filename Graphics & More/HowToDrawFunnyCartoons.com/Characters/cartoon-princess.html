How to draw a cartoon princess





This easy tutorial will help you learn to draw a cartoon princess! Every little girl in the world has, at one point or another, dream to become a nice and beautiful princess!

That's why many movies and toys are created using this particular subject. So what is a princess exactly? Simply a beautiful girl dressed with nice clothes and wearing a crown. Let's try to draw a simple princess in only nine easy steps!

The first step should be pretty easy! Just draw a plain circle to illustrate the head of your character.

Next, draw three rectangles to create the neck, the body and the belt of your princess. Don't draw rectangles that are too big! With want to build a princess that is thin and delicate!

Now it's time to add the arms. Just sketch two rectangles and a circle for each arm. Of course, the circle will be used to form the hands of your character.

This step is the last one that we will add some basic shapes. First, draw a triangle to create the dress. Then, go a little higher and add the crown of your princess by drawing a rectangle. Finally, just add a circle for the hair. We will work on these shapes on the next step.

Since all basic shapes are now added, it'S now time to refine our drawing. Work on the outline of the head of your character. Make the chin narrow and the forehead large and almost flat. You can also work on the crown to give it a more enjoyable shape. Draw some long hair, just like a princess should have!

Now the body! Draw a small waist and a tiny belt. Also make sure that the hands are tiny and delicate.

The bottom of the dress should be easy to do. To save us a little work, the shoes of the princess should be hidden!

Finally, add some details on the face of your princess by drawing the eyes, the pupils, the nose and the mouth.

When you are done, just add some colors to make your princess more enjoyable and cute! Like you can see, drawing a cartoon princess should not be a big issue if you already know how to draw a girl! Just make sure she is dressed nicely and properly and that's it! You now have a cute princess that is ready to conquer the heart of a charming prince in the fantasy story of your choice! Have fun!
