How to draw a cartoon bunny





This simple tutorial will help you learn to draw a cartoon bunny. So you might ask yourself: What is the difference between a rabbit and a bunny? Well, there is no real differences! "Bunny" is just another way to call a rabbit... period!

But when you think of a bunny, you might think of a more rounder, cuter and smoother animal than a rabbit. And that is precisely what we will try to do in this tutorial! Drawing a cute and funny rabbit... well I mean, bunny!

First, draw a nice round circle to illustrate the head. On top of this new shape, add two small rectangles to create the base of the ears. Once you are finished, draw two large rectangles on top of the previous shapes to form the ears. Make sure that your rectangles are placed just like shown in the image above!

Next, draw a rectangle to illustrate the body. We will work on this shape later to make it more enjoyable to the eyes! After that, sketch two large rectangles over the body to illustrate the feet of your character. Like you can see above, we are gonna draw a bunny that is seated.

This step is rather easy! Just add two rectangles to form the hands of your bunny!

Now it's time to refine our little bunny. Work on the outline of the ears and make the bottom part really thin and narrow. The main part of the ears should be large and round.

Once you are done, work on the outline of the feet of your character. You can add three little toes on each of them. The bottom part of the feet should be narrower.

The body of our cartoon bunny should be thin and average in size. You can also work on the outline of the hands to make them more enjoyable to watch!

And now, for the last step before adding some colors, just add all the details remaining. You can draw the inside of the ears. Add the nose, the eyes and the mouth of your rabbit (or bunny!).

Well, it wasn't too hard to draw, wasn't it? This cute bunny is really a charm to illustrate if you manage to follow all steps carefully and patiently. Remember to practice often and one day, you will be able to draw this adorable creature all by yourself, without looking even a second at this easy tutorial! Have fun!
