How to draw cartoons: miscellaneous tutorials





Now that you know how to draw cartoons a little better, why not try to create a greater variety of objects, animals and other creatures?

In this part of the website, you will find miscellaneous items and characters that did not fit in the sections above.



From fairies, to trees, to tribal flames, if you did not find what you were looking for yet, you just might find it here. Don't hesitate to use the contact form (under the "contact me" button on your left!) to suggest new tutorials that I could add to this website. I am always open to suggestions!

My website is constantly being updated so come back often to check out the new lessons!

And, if you feel safe enough to go one step further, you can always take a look at the vector part of this website. It will give you precious information to start using a vector software to give your creation a more professional look. And, from there, who knows what could happen!

Start practicing now by choosing your favorite subject on your right!
